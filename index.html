About Programme

What is K-Youth?

Everything you need to know about the K-Youth Development Programme 2026 — eligibility, costs, structure, and more.

What is the K-Youth Development Programme 2026?
Click to reveal answer
Answer
K-Youth is a youth employability initiative by Khazanah Nasional Berhad aimed at developing Malaysia's future talents through industry-relevant skills and real-world experience.
What are the eligibility requirements?
Click to reveal answer
Answer
Malaysian citizen, aged 18–30, currently unemployed, Diploma/Degree holder, no prior government programmes (MySTEP, PROTÉGÉ, etc.), able to commit full-time for 4 months, with interest in Digital & Technology.
What is the cost of the programme?
Click to reveal answer
Answer
There is no cost to participants. In fact, you will receive a monthly allowance of approximately RM 2,000 throughout the programme.
What is the overall programme structure?
Click to reveal answer
Answer
The programme runs for 4 months: 1 month of hybrid training followed by 3 months of On-the-Job Training (OJT). OJT is conducted fully on-site.
Will I receive a certificate upon completion?
Click to reveal answer
Answer
Yes! Participants who successfully complete the programme will receive industry-recognised certifications from Microsoft, Google, or AWS.
When will I receive my offer/placement letter?
Click to reveal answer
Answer
Offer letters will be issued after final approval. Further details, including placement host company information, will be included in the letter.
Are host companies under Pepper Labs?
Click to reveal answer
Answer
No. Host companies are external organisations. If your preferred company is not listed, they will need to register under the programme to participate.
Are there placements outside Klang Valley?
Click to reveal answer
Answer
Currently, placements are only available within Klang Valley. Updates will be shared if opportunities outside the area become available.

OJT Experience

What is On-the-Job Training (OJT)?

OJT is a minimum 15-week paid workplace placement where participants gain real industry experience and apply their newly acquired knowledge and skills.

Will I get a job after the programme?
Click to reveal answer
Answer
Employment depends on your performance and the host company's hiring needs. Strong performance during OJT significantly increases your chances of being retained.
Is accommodation provided during OJT?
Click to reveal answer
Answer
No, accommodation is not provided by the programme. However, some host companies may offer arrangements — you can enquire about this during interviews.
Where is the physical class & OJT location?
Click to reveal answer
Answer
Classroom sessions are held in Petaling Jaya. OJT placement details will be shared exclusively with successful candidates after selection.
