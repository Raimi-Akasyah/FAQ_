Frequently Asked Questions
// Question – Answer
Here you can find answers to the most frequently asked questions.
What is the K-Youth Development Programme 2026?
Answer:
K-Youth Development Programme is a youth employability initiative by Khazanah Nasional Berhad aimed at developing Malaysia's future talents through industry-relevant skills and experience.
What are the requirements?
Answer:
Malaysian citizen aged 18–30 · Currently unemployed · Diploma or Degree holder · Not participated in similar government programmes (MySTEP, PROTÉGÉ, etc.) · Able to commit full-time for 4 months · Strong interest in Digital & Technology roles.
What is the cost of the programme?
Answer:
There is no cost. Participants will receive a monthly allowance of approximately RM2,000.
What is the programme structure?
Answer:
The programme runs for 4 months: 1 month of training followed by 3 months of On-the-Job Training (OJT). Training is hybrid, while OJT is fully physical.
Will I receive a certificate?
Answer:
Yes, participants will receive industry-recognised certificates upon successful completion.
When will I receive my offer / placement letter?
Answer:
It will be issued after final approval. Further details will be included in your offer letter.
Are host companies under Pepper Labs?
Answer:
No, host companies are external. If a company is not listed, they will need to register under the programme to participate.
Are there placements outside Klang Valley?
Answer:
Currently, there are no placements outside Klang Valley. Updates will be shared if opportunities become available.
What is the course structure?
Answer:
The programme focuses on Artificial Intelligence (AI) training with placement opportunities, allowing participants to apply AI solutions in real-world business environments.
When can I register for this programme?
Answer:
Registration is currently open. Training is expected to begin in April 2026.
What are the training & reporting dates?
Answer:
Bootcamp: 15–19 April. Reporting date: 22 April.
What will I learn in this programme?
Answer:
Technical: AI Certifications (Microsoft, Google, AWS)
Professional: Resume Writing, Interview & Communication Skills
Future Skills: ESG, Green Skills, Cybersecurity
Leadership: Corporate Leadership, Public Policy Awareness
What is the mode of learning?
Answer:
The programme uses hybrid learning including physical classes, virtual sessions, webinars, mentorship, and self-paced learning modules.
What happens if I get a job during the programme?
Answer:
You are encouraged to pursue job opportunities. If you secure a permanent role, the programme will assist your transition.
Is placement onsite or remote?
Answer:
The majority of positions require on-site attendance, subject to the host company.
How much is the penalty fee?
Answer:
The withdrawal fee varies case by case, based on incurred course fees and salaries disbursed.
What is On-the-Job Training (OJT)?
Answer:
OJT is a minimum 15-week paid placement where participants gain real workplace experience and apply their knowledge.
Will I get a job after the programme?
Answer:
Employment depends on your performance and company hiring needs, but strong performance increases your chances significantly.
Is accommodation provided during OJT?
Answer:
No, accommodation is not provided. However, some companies may offer arrangements during interviews.
Where is the physical class & OJT location?
Answer:
Classroom sessions are held in Petaling Jaya. OJT details will be shared with successful candidates only.
